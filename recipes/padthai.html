Chicken Pad Thai

Classic but so good Pad Thaï !

Ingredients:

  • 1 (12 ounce) package rice noodles
  • 1 pound chicken drimsticks cut into bite-sized pieces
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • ⅛ tablespoon crushed red pepper
  • 2 cups bean sprouts
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • ¼ cup crushed peanuts
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  • 5 chives, big chops

Steps:

  1. Soak rice noodles in cold water until soft, 30 to 50 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Meanwhile, heat oil in a wok; add chicken and sauté until browned. Remove chicken and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in the wok over medium-high heat. Crack eggs into hot oil and cook until firm. Stir in chicken and chives and cook for 5 minutes. Add softened noodles, sugar, fish sauce, vinegar, and red pepper; mix well until noodles are tender. Adjust seasonings to taste. Stir in bean sprouts and cook for 3 minutes.
  4. Serve topped with green onions, crushed peanuts, and a wedge of lemon.
